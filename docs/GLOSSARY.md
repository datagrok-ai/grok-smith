# Glossary

- **SEND**: Standard for Exchange of Nonclinical Data. CDISC standard for organizing animal study data.
- **Domain**: In SEND, a domain is a category of data (e.g., BW = Body Weights, CL = Clinical Observations, LB = Lab Results, MI = Microscopic Findings, MA = Macroscopic Findings).
- **Study**: A defined experiment or trial, typically involving test subjects (animals in SEND context).
- **Subject**: An individual animal in a study, identified by USUBJID.
- **Test Article**: The compound being evaluated in a study.
- **Batch**: A specific physical preparation of a compound, identified by lot number.
- **GLP**: Good Laboratory Practice. Regulatory compliance framework for nonclinical studies.
- **CDISC**: Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium. Organization that maintains SEND and other data standards.
